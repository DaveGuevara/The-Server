Based on real life experience, The Server depicts the adventures of Matt (the author) as a Process Server in the city of New York."

Cast

About

Throughout his journey, Matt discovers and explores various cultures and ethnicities. His position at work demands quick results and comes with challenges that force him to be persistent, creative and competitive.

His relationship with each character illustrated is tied to his job and it's affairs. They represent the same diversity of the people that he comes in contact with during his travels while working; making Matt realize that a book should not be judge by its cover.

Tim

Server

30 year old irish born male with a Jamaican accent, whose method of serving papers is forcing people to take them by putting fear in their hearts. He loves to drink Guinness Stout and listen to Bob Marley songs and falls asleep everytime he gets on the train. He is always talking and plotting to eat Kanal's rabbit served up with potatoes and cabbage.

Kanal

The Boss

A metrosexual male from India who was raised in New York, He loves clothes, great food and fine wine. He Always has a song to sing and never seems down about life. His life is consumed by the materialistic things of the world and keeping himself groomed. He is in his mid 40's and is like the Big Brother/Father of the company. He is also brain washed by his pet that controls his decisions on who he sends out to serve papers and what area they should go. His mission is to destroy Matt by really desn't know why he hates him.

Frankie

IT Analyst

Mid 20's British male who has a hippie personality and loves to smoke Hokkahs and blow things up. He is the brains behind making Matt's gadgets for his travels out serving papers. He also moon lights at night playing the druns for a rock band and also has a big crush on Lizzette and hates that she likes Matt.

Matt

Server

Mid 20's male from Brooklyn, the youngest and the rookie of the company, he has a care free attitude about life and gets along with everyone and adapts to any environment. He is always trying to fix people and things around him and always seems to get out of rough situations with the help of Frankie and his imaginary friend. He likes Lizzette and knows that the rabbit can talk and is controlling Kanal. He is a master of disguise and the best server in the company, but feels like someone is always following him and out to get him.

Lizette

Receptionist

Late 20's latin woman, who is sexy and voluptous and is sterotyped as a typical beautiful woman with no brain, she has a nervous problem that makes her burp when she talks except when she is around Matt. She also understands morse code and is the only one in the office that can relate to Vincetti when he calls in. Always on the phone with her girlfriend talking about Kanal and why he stays locked up in his office with the rabbit and takes him everywhere.

Vincetti

Server

Early 30's Italian male that seems to be always eating and never speaks, his only form of communicating is morse code to Lizette. He also seems to pop up at every location that Matt is at after finishing a service.

GL

Server

20 year old Korean male born and raised in Harlem. He is heavy into Hip Hop and is groomed by his Environment which makes it seem like he does not like his own background. He is loud and very militant and does not get along with Kanal, his approach to serving papers is tricking people about the papers he has for them, and he also likes Lizette.
